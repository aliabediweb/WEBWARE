Webware is an American technology startup company based in the Silicon Valley region of California. Founded by Ali Abedi in 2020, the company went from modifying computer parts to becoming a full scale hardware manufacturer. What started out in a garage now has a headquarters, a manufacturing plant, and a customer service center.

We aim to deliver the world's most innovative technology. All of our products are developed to be more efficient than what is currently on the market. We offer high performance CPUs, mother boards, graphics cards, memory drives, optical drives, and cooling solutions.
